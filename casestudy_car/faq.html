What will happen if my car is financed by a bank and I want to sell it to CARBEE?

If the car you want to sell is financed, simply bring along a current dated foreclosure letter issued by the bank and we will pay the loan on your car. The settlement amount will be deducted from the CARBEE quote for your car. Alternatively, you can settle the loan yourself before selling the car to us. CARBEE may hold back a certain amount until the bank loan clearance NOC is submitted by you.

Your website quote does not match my expectations. Why should I visit your branch?

The website quote is an estimated value range generated by our pricing algorithm based on some broad parameters like car make and model, variant, odometer reading and other details that you enter on the website. The final price quote offered also takes into consideration the physical condition of your vehicle as evaluated during the inspection process and market conditions.

I cannot find my car’s registration state while booking the appointment online.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can change your appointment time, date or branch. Simply login with your registered mobile number on our website or mobile app and visit the ‘My Appointment’ section. There is also a link to reschedule in the appointment confirmation email that you receive. Alternatively, you can call our customer helpline number at 1800 258 5656 and we will reschedule your appointment for you.

Is it mandatory to book an appointment?

Yes. By booking an appointment with us, you are reserving your dedicated slot to be catered to by one of our associates at the branch. In case, you drop in at any of our branches without having an appointment booked, you will be asked to book one on the spot. This will lead to unnecessary wastage of time and efforts which we tend to avoid. We are serving several customers at a time and our goal is to ensure all of them receive the same kind of experience. Having dedicated slots booked in advance helps us achieve the same and allows us to serve our customers in the best way possible.

How do I book an appointment?

It’s simple! Log on to our website, fill in the basic details about your car as mentioned above and schedule an appointment at your nearest CARS24 branch. OR, you can simply call our customer helpline number at 1800-258-5656 and we will schedule an appointment for you.
Will you charge me for my car's inspection?

No, CARBEE inspection process at all our retail branches is absolutely free.
Why is there a difference between the online valuation and the actual price offered at the branch?

The online valuation indicates the estimated range you can get for your car considering it is in a picture-perfect condition. The detailed inspection conducted at the branch which determines the physical health and working of the car’s components decides what the final quote offered will be. The factors that impact the value of your car include the car make, aesthetics and mechanical condition, odometer reading and market conditions.
What all do you check during the inspection of my car?

Our used car inspection process is fair and thorough to ensure an objective standardized report that gives a clear view of the condition of your car. We start with a document check and test drive and then use the latest tools to assess the exterior, tires, engine, transmission, interiors and electricals. We also inspect the steering mechanism, suspension, brakes and accessories of your car during the test drive. Feel free to ask any questions from our team at the branch.
How long will my car's inspection take?

At CARBEE, we evaluate a used car on the basis of several parameters. Our dedicated car experts are trained to complete the entire process of car inspection within a time period of 30-45 minutes. However, there might be instances, pertaining to different car conditions, when the car inspection process may even extend to 90 minutes.
I got an SMS to check in, where do I have to check in at the branch?

One of our branch team members will come to you and assist you with the check-in process, provided you have booked an appointment beforehand. Please ensure that you get the check-in done as soon as you enter the branch so that you are queued in for the inspection process to start.
Till when are your branches open?

Our branches generally operate from 10 am - 6 pm. We recommend you to reach our branch by 5 pm to avoid any hassle or delay. Please note that there might be some branches across India which do not operate on a certain day of the week. When you try booking an appointment for a time slot, you can view if your preferred branch is operational on that particular time and day or not.
Can I send my driver to get the inspection done?

Yes, you can. However, you must ensure that your driver carries all the required documents to get the car inspected. Also, please note that the owner of the car needs to self-attest all the documents involved. We will also need the bank account details of the registered owner of the car to initiate the transaction.
What documents should I carry to the branch for my car's inspection?

It is important that you bring the following documents with you to proceed with the inspection of your car:

  1. Original RC (Mandatory)
  2. Insurance Certificate / Cover Note / Policy
  3. Original Invoice
  4. Duplicate Keys
  5. Service Manual
  6. Last Service Receipt
  7. Extended Warranty Certificate (if applicable)

To avail instant payment for your car, please bring along the following documents:

  1. PAN card of the registered owner (Mandatory for Payment)
  2. 4 Self-attested photos of the registered owner
  3. Registered owner's - Aadhar Card/ Pan Card/ Passport
  4. Financial documents in case of financed car
  5. Cancelled Cheque
  6. GSTIN Registration Certificate (Required only if the car's RC is in the name of the Firm / Company / HUF etc.)
Do remember to take out all of your personal belongings from your car before your inspection!
