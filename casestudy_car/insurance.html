Insurance Prices

Insurance covers the bills of vehicle repairs due to the damage caused during the time of an accident. It also covers the damage caused to your vehicle due to any reason other than an accident like theft, fire, etc.

Basic

₹10,000 / month

  • Can be claimed 2 times
  • Does not cover natural calamities
  • Covers only the body of the car

Pro

₹15,000 / month

  • Can be claimed 5 times
  • Covers some natural calamities
  • Third-Party handling of the car

Premium

₹20,000 / month

  • Can be claimed 10 times
  • Covers all natural calamities
  • Direct handling of the car
